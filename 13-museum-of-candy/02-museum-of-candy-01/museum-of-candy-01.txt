Museum of Candy Part 01
-----------------------
- A code-along project using Bootstrap
    - Create a fictional Instragram "museum" website

    - It is minimal & responsive website
        - Using light pastel colors
        - Includes large royalty-free photos, forcibly squared

        - Navigation bar collapse into a hamburger icon

        - Content stacks on top of each other
            - Image over section content
            - Section icon disappears
