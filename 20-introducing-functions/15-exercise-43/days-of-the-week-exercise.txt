Exercise 43: Days Of The Week Exericse
--------------------------------------
- Write a function called "returnDay"
    - Takes in one (1) parameter
        - A number from 1 to 7

    - Returns the day of the week
        - e.g. 1 = Monday

    - If the the number
        - Is less than 1 or greater than 7
        - Return a value of "null"

    - Hint:
        - Store the days of the week in an array
        - Or use an object w/ numerical keys
