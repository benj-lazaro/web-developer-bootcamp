Logical AND
-----------
- Logical operators allows to combine different expressions
    - To combine more than 1 piece of logic together 
        - To form 1 larger piece of logic


- JavaScript's 3 core Logical operators
    - Logical AND   = &&
    - Logical OR    = ||
    - Logical NOT   = !
    

- Logical AND
    - Requires for BOTH expressions to be true
        - In order for the entire expression to be true

    - If the lefthand expression is false
        - The entire expression is false
        - This is referred as "short circuiting"
