Lexical Scope
-------------
- A Lexical scope refers to constants and/or variables declared w/in
    - A nested child function that has access to 
        - The constants and/or variables that are declared w/in
        - The scope of the parent (or ancestor) function

    - IMPORTANT: The parent (or ancestor) function does NOT
        - Have access to the constants and/or variables that are
        - Declared w/in the child or descendant function(s)
