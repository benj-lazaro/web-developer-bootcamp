The Lovely for..of Loop
-----------------------
- The "for..of" loop 
    - It is a relatively new feature in JavaScript
        - NOT supported in Internet Explorer (IE)

    - Operates on a sequence of values
        - Sourced from an iterable object

    - Offers an nice & easy way to iterate over arrays or
        - Other iteratble objects (e.g. object, string)

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for...of)


Syntax:

for (const <singular_iterable_name> of <iteratble_object>) {
    ...
}
