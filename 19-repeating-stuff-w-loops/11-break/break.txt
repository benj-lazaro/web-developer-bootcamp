The Break Keyword
-----------------
- The "break" (keyword or) statement
    - Terminates the current loop or "switch" statement
        - Commonly used with a "while" loop

    - Transfer program control to the statement following the "break" statement

    - It can also be used to jump past a "labeled statement"
    
    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/break)


Syntax:

break;
break <label>;
