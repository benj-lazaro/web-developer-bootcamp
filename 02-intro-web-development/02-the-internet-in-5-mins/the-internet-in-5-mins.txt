The Internet in 5 Minutes
-------------------------
- High-level overview on how the Internet works
    - It is a bunch of inter-connected devices
        - e.g. desktop, laptop, mobile devices, Internet-enabled devices

    - It is a global network of networks
        - Connected using physical devices via routing

        - Specific physical devices whose job
            - Is to exchange data with another physical devices
            - e.g. routers, switches, servers

        - These individual devices are identified through their IP addresses
            - IP = Internet Protocol
        
    - It is the infrastructure that carries services like
        - E-mail
        - Web
        - File sharing
        - Online gaming
        - Streaming services
