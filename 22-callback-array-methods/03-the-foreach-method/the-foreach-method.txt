The "forEach" Method
--------------------
- The method "forEach()"
    - Accepts a callback function
    - Executes the function once per element in an array 

    - NOTE: This method is commonly used BEFORE
        - The advent of the "for..of" loop


Syntax:
<array_name>.forEach(<callback_function>);

<array_name>.forEach(<callback_function>, <this_argument_value>);
